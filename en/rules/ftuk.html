Daily loss: the rule that kills the most accounts

FTUK lets you lose up to 4% in a single day. If your equity (floating included) touches that floor, the account is gone — even if it recovers afterwards. It's the limit that quietly blows up the most accounts, because one big trade or a bad-day streak is enough.

Maximum drawdown (8% relative)

FTUK's total loss cap is 8%. Being relative/trailing, the floor follows you up: careful, because you can blow up even while green relative to the start.

On FTUK's flagship program, consistency does not block the evaluation.
