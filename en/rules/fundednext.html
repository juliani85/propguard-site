Daily loss: the rule that kills the most accounts

FundedNext lets you lose up to 5% in a single day. If your equity (floating included) touches that floor, the account is gone — even if it recovers afterwards. It's the limit that quietly blows up the most accounts, because one big trade or a bad-day streak is enough.

Maximum drawdown (10% static)

FundedNext's total loss cap is 10%. Being static, the floor doesn't rise when you win: it's always measured against the initial balance. That's good — it gives you room as you grow.

Consistency

On FundedNext's flagship program, consistency does not block the evaluation. CONFIRMED (help center): the 40% rule does NOT apply to CFD Stellar accounts (2-Step/1-Step/Lite/Instant) — neither in challenge nor funded. Only applies to Futures (Bolt/Legacy).

Quick Strike (the 30-second rule)

This is FundedNext's silent trap: if a large share of your profit comes from trades closed in under 30 seconds, they can void the account. Warning from 20%, breach at 30%. It's the easiest rule to break without noticing if you scalp fast.
